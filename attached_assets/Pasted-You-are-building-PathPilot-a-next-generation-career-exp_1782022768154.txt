You are building PathPilot, a next-generation career exploration platform that helps students discover careers through immersive simulations, behavioral analysis, and personalized opportunity recommendations.

This product should feel like a premium, venture-backed technology platform, not a student project, educational worksheet, generic dashboard, or template-generated SaaS.

Core Product Concept

Most students choose careers based on assumptions, social media, quizzes, or secondhand advice.

PathPilot allows students to experience realistic career scenarios before making decisions about their future.

Students progress through simulations, make decisions under realistic constraints, experience consequences, and receive career recommendations based on observed behavior rather than self-reported preferences.
